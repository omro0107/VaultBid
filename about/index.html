About us

VaultBid is a premier auction platform founded in 2003 and headquartered in Oslo. We connect a global community of buyers and sellers, offering a seamless space to trade unique and valuable items. With a focus on transparency and trust, VaultBid has become the go-to destination for art, design, collectibles, and beyond.

The Story of Vaultbid

EST. 2003

Our journey began in Oslo with a vision to revolutionize the auction experience. Since day one, VaultBid has been dedicated to empowering individuals to share their treasures with the world. Over the years, we’ve grown from a local platform to an international marketplace, building a reputation for quality, innovation, and integrity.

Our Culture

At VaultBid, we believe in the power of community and connection. We’re driven by a passion for discovery, a commitment to sustainability, and a dedication to fostering meaningful exchanges. Whether you're a seasoned collector or a curious newcomer, our platform is designed to inspire, engage, and support you every step of the way.
